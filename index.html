About

The Operation Spark Alumni Board is a group of Operation Spark Immersion program graduates who are committed to supporting the students of Operation Spark through every phase of the program; from info session to immersion, first job hunt to next job hunt.

Our Mission

We, as an autonomous organization separate from Operation Spark, should responsibly grow and strengthen the Operation Spark Alumni Network, wherever possible, pre-immersion and post-immersion.

We strive to facilitate opportunities for the underrepresented and underserved, and make sure graduates are getting appropriate compensation for their skill levels.
